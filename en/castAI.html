CAST AI: Cloud Optimization Roadmap

Click on each stage to see the evolution of automated FinOps

1. Problem Discovery (2019) +
  • Funding Seed capital from founders (serial entrepreneurs Yuri Frayman, Leon Kuperman, and Laurent Gil).
  • Strategic Moves Identifying that Kubernetes management is too complex for humans to optimize manually, leading to massive "cloud waste" (over-provisioning).
  • Goal Solving the multi-cloud complexity and automated cost reduction for containerized applications.
2. Solution Exploration +
  • Funding Strategic Seed investment.
  • Strategic Moves Developing an AI engine that can analyze Kubernetes clusters and suggest (initially) and then execute (automatically) the most cost-efficient instance types in real-time.
  • MVP A "Free Savings Report" tool that allowed DevOps teams to see exactly how much they could save before even installing the platform.
3. Product–Market Fit (2020-2021) +
  • Funding $10M Series A led by WestCap.
  • Strategic Moves Transitioning from simple reporting to "Autonomous Optimization." Integration with AWS, GCP, and Azure.
  • Traction Rapid adoption by high-growth startups and digital-native companies where cloud costs were spiraling out of control.
4. Scaling & Operational Excellence +
  • Funding $20M Series B (2023) led by Creandum.
  • Strategic Moves Expanding the product line to include automated security and disaster recovery. Launching "Spot Instance" automation, allowing companies to use the cheapest cloud resources with 100% reliability.
  • Revenue Streams Tiered SaaS pricing based on a percentage of savings or a flat fee per node.
5. Hyper-Growth (2024-Present) +
  • Funding $35M investment from Vintage Investment Partners and existing backers.
  • Strategic Moves Targeting Enterprise-level clients. Shifting the narrative from just "saving money" to "enabling AI scaling" (optimizing expensive GPU clusters for AI training).
  • Market Impact CAST AI now manages clusters for global leaders, often reducing their infrastructure spend by over 60% automatically.
